Your expemption registrations

Your account holds all the exemptions that you have registered.

You should make sure you read the guidance before registering your exemption.

You can save your progress and return later via this portal.

The exemption details will be logged onto the register as soon as you submit it.

Enforcing authorities will have access to the register to allow them to check the details you have provided.

As this is a self-certification register, you do no tneed to do anything else. The Enforcing Authority will only contact you if they have a query about the exemption you have registered.

We will send you a notification 28 days before your exemption expires.
