Upload your recommendations report (usually an Energy Performance Certificate (EPC))

You must provide the energy efficiency recommendations report for the property being registered. This is usually an EPC. If you don't have an EPC, a Green Deal report or surveryor report can also be used.

If your EPC is available online

You can use the 'Find an energy certificate' (opens in new window) tool to provide a link to the EPC report for this property.

If your EPC is not available online, or you're uploading a different report

You must upload a copy of the energy efficiency recommendations report for this property. Files must be either png, pdf, jpg, jpeg, doc or docx. The file can not be larger than 4MB.
