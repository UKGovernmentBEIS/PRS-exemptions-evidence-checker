Upload your Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)

You must provide an EPC for the property being registered.

If your EPC is available online

You can use the '[Find an energy certificate](https://www.gov.uk/find-energy-certificate)' (opens in new window) tool to provide a link to the EPC report for this property.

If your EPC is not available online

You must upload a copy of the EPC for this property. Files must be either png, pdf, jpg, jpeg, doc or docx. The file can not be larger than 4MB.
